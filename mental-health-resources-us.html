Crisis Hotline Phone Numbers

SAMHSA’s National Helpline= 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
TTY (for deaf, hard of hearing, or speech-impaired)= 1-800-487-4889
National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255); En español 1-888-628-9454
Crisis Text Line: Text “HELLO” to 741741
Veterans Crisis Line: Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255) and press 1 or text to 838255
Disaster Distress Helpline: Call or text 1-800-985-5990
NAMI HelpLine: 1-800-950-NAMI (6264) [Monday through Friday, 10 a.m.–8 p.m., ET.]
Emergency Line: 911
