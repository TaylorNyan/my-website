Support Groups (In-person and online)

  • +SGSupportGroup lets people make anonymous posts looking for advice or support from others struggling with similar mental illness symptoms.

  • NAMI Connection is a weekly recovery support group for people living with mental illness in which people learn from each others’ experiences, share coping strategies, and offer each other encouragement and understanding.

  • NAMI Family Connection free, confidential and safe groups of families helping other families who live with mental health challenges by utilizing their collective lived experiences and learned group wisdom.

  • Smart Recovery helps one another resolve problems with any addiction (to drugs or alcohol or to activities such as gambling or over-eating). Has both online and in-person meetings!

  • Grief Share has seminars and support groups that are led by people who understand what you are going through and want to help. They have free daily encouragement emails when you sign up as well to keep you motivated!

  • Depression and Bipolar Support Alliance give people living with depression and bipolar disorder a safe, welcoming place to share experiences, discuss coping skills, and offer each other hope.

  • Support Groups Central provides various support group meetings varying from depression, parenting, weight management etc.

  • Health Unlocked like SGSupportGroup, lets you post anonymously and discusses anxiety and depression to others who truly understand.

  • Postpartum Support International supports parents and upcoming parents with multiple stresses and connects with others in similar situations such as military parents, NICU parents, etc.

  • Daily Strength forums for those that want to share your experience and connect with those on the same path.

  • 7 Cups not only provides therapy; they provide chat rooms for multiple situations along with volunteer listeners
